ELECTRICAL BOX

Task: Redesign an electrical box to be representative of its surroundings. Turn it into an experience.

I chose an electrical box near Newtonville. It is placed next to an intersection that people often stop to wait for. I wanted to create a typographic design with some panels easy to read from a distance or if passing quickly. Some take more time to explore.

I chose colors that echo the surrounding nature, while standing out and offering words to contemplate.

Tools: Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop
