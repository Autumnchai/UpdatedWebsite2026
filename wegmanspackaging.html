GROCERY PACKAGING

Task: Create a system of packaging for a grocery store

For this Wegmans brand, I created a hypothetical new system of packaging based on original illustrations. My illustrations were influenced by the Arts and Crafts movement as a nod to the humble, organic family-run business.

I wanted the products to feel unified.

Tools: Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop, Procreate
Materials: cardstock
