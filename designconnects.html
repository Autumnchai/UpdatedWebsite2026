DESIGN CONNECTS

Task: Create an entry for the 7th Emirates International Poster Festival 2025 with the theme Bridging Cultures, Building Communities. My piece Interlaced was selected for the show.

Concept: I wanted to show how cultures of the world are unique and united through design. I considered how we use design to share messages, histories, and aesthetics. Different textures, patterns, and colors are used in different places, but we all belong to the same patchwork.

Tools: Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Indesign
Materials: scanner, string & fabric
