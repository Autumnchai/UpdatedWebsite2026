CORPSE BLOOM
ALBUM & ASSETS

CORPSE BLOOM is a multi-sensory expression of my experiences with beauty, change, and grief. Original gritty sounds and textures, paired with recyclable, delicate materials, capture the reality of decomposition over time. Seed paper in every CD booklet reminds us that wherever there are endings, there are also new beginnings.

This project is a reminder & challenge to myself and others to cherish the temporary, reconsider arbitrary expectations, and embrace transformation.
