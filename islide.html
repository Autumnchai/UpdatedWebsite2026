STAR SIGNS COLLECTIONS

Task: During my internship at ISlide USA I created collections of in-house designs available for customers to purchase.

For the first star sign collection, I created original illustrations that capture the elegance of each well-known star sign symbol in a style that appeals to a young adult demographic.

For the second star sign collection, I created hand-rendered typography designs with words of the signs made of their associated earth elements.

I also created marketing assets for the Instagram, and web banners for promotion on their website IslideUSA.com

Tools: Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Indesign, Procreate
